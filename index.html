What is PyCaribbean About?

PyCaribbean is the first Python Conference of its kind in the Caribbean. Our goal is to connect the North American and European Developer Community with the Hispanic and Caribbean community.

This will be the third installment, and one we’re sure you won’t forget!

PyCaribbean will be held at Pyhex Work, the biggest coworking space of the Caribbean.
